# Join Us

*The Digital Heart Research Group is part of the [Engineering Medicine Research Group](https://emg-nju.github.io/Homepage/) at the Medical School of Nanjing University.*

## 中文招聘信息

长期招聘博士后、助理研究员、科研助理，招收博士或硕士研究生、访问学生及本科生。欢迎对**医工交叉、数字医学、心血管疾病、人工智能**等相关领域感兴趣的同学加入，共创数字孪生驱动的精准医学新模式。

**重点招聘招生领域**（博士后、博士、硕士、科研助理；也可推荐至附属鼓楼医院）：

- **专业方向**：计算机、电子信息、生物医学工程等医工交叉学科。在心脏数字孪生、复杂器官系统与疾病数字建模、深度学习驱动复杂系统模型代理、基于深度学习的因果推理、或虚拟现实技术等相关领域有经验者优先。
- **AI 分析与数字孪生构建方向**：深度学习代理模型（Surrogate Model）开发；基于深度学习的因果推理方法解析复杂病理机制；跨学科协作。
- **多器官数字孪生建模方向**：心脏数字孪生模型开发；高性能计算与算法实现；多器官模型集成与验证，跨学科协作。

**联系方式：** hni (at) nju.edu.cn

## In English

We are actively recruiting curious, motivated people to help build digital twins of the human heart and use them to understand and treat cardiovascular disease. We welcome applicants from **diverse educational backgrounds** — computer science, electronic & information engineering, biomedical engineering, physics, applied mathematics, mechanical engineering, physiology, pharmacology, cardiology, and clinical medicine.

Prior experience in any of the following is a plus:

- Heart digital twins and complex-system modeling of disease
- Deep-learning surrogate models for physics-based simulators
- Causal inference for mechanistic understanding of disease
- Virtual / augmented reality for clinical and scientific applications
- High-performance computing for biomedical simulation

**Contact:** hni (at) nju.edu.cn

## Postdoctoral Fellows

Postdoctoral positions are open to candidates with (or expecting) a Ph.D. in a relevant field. Strong computational, mathematical, or experimental backgrounds are all welcome.

To apply, please email **hni (at) nju.edu.cn** with:

- A brief statement of research interests (1 page)
- CV with publication list
- Names and contact details of 2–3 references

---

## Ph.D. and Master's Students

Prospective graduate students apply through the standard admission channels of Nanjing University. If you are interested in joining the group, please email **hni (at) nju.edu.cn** in advance — ideally before applying — with your CV and a short note describing your background and interests.

We also co-supervise students with affiliated hospitals and departments, including **Nanjing Drum Tower Hospital**.

## Research Assistants & Technical Staff

We recruit research assistants and technical staff in two broad areas:

**AI / digital-twin analytics**
- Deep-learning surrogate models
- Causal-inference methods for complex pathology
- Cross-disciplinary collaboration

**Multi-organ digital-twin modeling**
- Heart digital-twin development
- High-performance computing and algorithmic implementation
